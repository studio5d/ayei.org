WHO WE ARE

Empowering Africa’s Next Generation of Digital Leaders

The AfriTech Youth Empowerment Initiative (AYEI) is a Pan-African non-profit organization committed to transforming Africa through technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

We bridge the digital divide by equipping young Africans with practical digital skills, structured mentorship, startup incubation, and global exposure — enabling them to build meaningful careers and scalable ventures.

🌍 Pan-African Reach
🚀 Innovation & Entrepreneurship Focus
🤝 Structured Mentorship Ecosystem

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and scale youth potential across Africa by building an ecosystem where innovation thrives and young leaders shape the continent’s digital future.

Our Approach

Through skills training, mentorship, incubation programs, and advocacy, we empower youth to become innovators, founders, and changemakers in the global digital economy.

Core Requirements for Volunteers

  1. Relevant Knowledge or Experience
    Demonstrate practical knowledge or hands-on experience in your chosen field.
  2. Educational or Training Background
    Possess a certificate, training, or verifiable learning related to the volunteer role.
  3. Intermediate Skill Level
    Maintain at least intermediate proficiency (entry-level applicants may join under mentorship pathways).
  4. Commitment & Reliability
    Be available for meetings, mentorship sessions, timelines, and assigned project deliverables.
  5. Professional Communication
    Meet deadlines, communicate proactively, and maintain professional conduct.
  6. Integrity & Respect
    Uphold ethical behavior, respect, and professionalism in all engagements.
  7. Team Collaboration
    Work effectively within structured teams and follow program leadership guidance.
  8. Mentorship Mindset
    Show patience and willingness to guide learners aged 13–35.
  9. Mission Alignment
    Support Afritech’s vision of empowering Africa through digital innovation.
  10. Growth & Learning Attitude
    Remain open to feedback, leadership development, and continuous improvement.
