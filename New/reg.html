WHO WE ARE

Empowering Africa’s Next Generation of Digital Leaders

The AfriTech Youth Empowerment Initiative (AYEI) is a Pan-African non-profit organization committed to transforming Africa through technology, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

We bridge the digital divide by equipping young Africans with practical digital skills, structured mentorship, startup incubation, and global exposure — enabling them to build meaningful careers and scalable ventures.

🌍 Pan-African Reach
🚀 Innovation & Entrepreneurship Focus
🤝 Structured Mentorship Ecosystem

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and scale youth potential across Africa by building an ecosystem where innovation thrives and young leaders shape the continent’s digital future.

Our Approach

Through skills training, mentorship, incubation programs, and advocacy, we empower youth to become innovators, founders, and changemakers in the global digital economy.
